Mission

Empowering fresh graduates with a streamlined platform that connects them to enriching fellowships, volunteer work, and internships, fostering their professional development and enabling meaningful contriburions to society.

Vision

To be the premier conduit between academia and the professional sphere ensuring every graduate can acquire practical experience, sharpen their skills, and realize their career dreams.

Our Story

The inception of GradConn was driven by our shared recognition of a significant gap in the transition from academic life to the professional world. As recent graduates ourselves, we experienced the firsthand the challenges of finding meaningful opportunities that aligned with our passions and career goals. We saw many individuals struggling to navigate this crucial phase and decided to create a solution. With diverse backgrounds in technology, design, and education, we came together with a common goal to create a platform that simplifies the job search process and provide graduates with opportunities to grow and make an impact. Our journey has been fueled by a commitment to helping others succeed, and we continue to innovate and improve our platform to better serve the needs of fresh graduates as close as it can be.
